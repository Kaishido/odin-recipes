Hamburger

Description

These burgers are the best on the grill in the summertime. Jam-packed with all kinds of stuff, and no tasteless bread crumbs!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill.
  2. Mix the beef with the seasoning.
  3. Cook the beef.
  4. Construct the sandwich with anything you want!
