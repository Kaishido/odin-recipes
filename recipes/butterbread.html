Butter Bread

Description

This bread is the perfect dessert for a party, as well as a tradition in the French West Indies. It'll pair well with hot chocolate and can also be eaten for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Dry Yeast
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt and butter together.
  2. Mix dry yeast with a bit of warm milk.
  3. Mix eggs, milk, water and the now activated yeast together.
  4. Make a dough, fold that dough 24 times on itself.
  5. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  6. Repeat step 4 and 5 2 more times and on the third time, let the dough rest for 40 minutes.
  7. Shape the dough in a creative way!
  8. Coat the shaped dough with egg yolk and vanilla extract.
  9. Bake it for 40 minutes at 355 degrees.
  10. Enjoy!
